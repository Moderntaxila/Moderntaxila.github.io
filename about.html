ABOUT MODERN TAXILA

Modern Taxila is well-known Institute of West Bengal, preparing candidates for the Civil Services Examination at all the three levels – Preliminary Test, Main Examination and Personality Test.
The Institute was founded in 2020 by Mr. Md Intekhab Alam Khan. Who had to his credit numerous academic distinctions from Indian Universities and abroad, including a diploma on Networking from CISCO. He has lot of experience of teaching History and Polity for Civil Service, he is also a historian and wrote number of books on competitive exams.
Since its inception the Institute has helped number students to enter the Civil Services especially in WBCS and other State and Central Services. We also provide preparations of SSC/Railways and Banking Exams. The teaching faculty of the Institute has been drawn from highly qualified and experienced teachers of the Central Universities and other reputed Institutes. To provide quality education and guidance normally a minimum of two or three teachers are engaged for each subject. In General Studies, some teachers are appointed for guiding the candidates.

Place of Functioning

The Institute imparts guidance at Kolkata only. The Institute does not have any branch office anywhere in India other than Kolkata for providing guidance for the Civil Services Examination, SSC/Railways and Banking Exams.
